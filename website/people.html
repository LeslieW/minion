Research Support

The MINION project was started at the University of St Andrews, School of Computer Science. Chris Jefferson moved to Oxford University on March 31, 2006.

Ian Miguel's work on Minion has been supported by a UK Royal Academy of Engineering/EPSRC Fellowship. Chris Jefferson and Karen Petrie's work at St Andrews has been supported by EPSRC Grant GR/S30580. Chris Jefferson's work at Oxford has been supported by EPSRC Grant EP/D032636. We are very grateful for this support.
